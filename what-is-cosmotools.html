Core Philosophy

Just pure tools.

Tools should be fast, private, and accessible to everyone. Unlike many online utilities that rely on servers and tracking scripts, CosmoTools processes everything directly in your browser.

Privacy First

No tracking scripts, no analytics trackers, no cookies for profiling, no hidden telemetry.

Your files and data never leave your device.

No Distractions

Many platforms overload pages with ads. We provide a clean, distraction-free experience.

  • - No intrusive ads
  • - No popup notifications
  • - No misleading buttons

Stateless Arch

Does not rely on server-side storage. All operations are performed locally using JS.

  • - Faster processing
  • - Fewer dependencies
  • - Long-term reliability
